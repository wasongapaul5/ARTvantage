OUR STORY

ARTvantage is a social enterprise facilitated by young artists, based in Nairobi, Kenya. We are a collective of both ladies and gentlemen, each from a different family across the continent. Together, through the art products that we create and are now able to sell, we are able to support a large number of people in our communities, and the impact of ARTvantage on our lives has been remarkable.

OUR REACH.

Our organization continues to expand and develop.We want to reach people in every corner of the space for us to nurture up talents at a tender stage for the better tommorow.We want to reach all the audience from different continents and moreover be part of the world's leading art gallery center. We want to change the face of our country and develop our creative skills through art.
