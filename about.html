OUR AIM.

We aim at diversifying our activities so as to accommodate different levels of skills and abilities.We want to reach out to the less fortunate and bring hope to the hopeless individuals around the society. We also aim at bringing transparency to an individual and getting to be creative in today's world. Artvantage strives to stand out with a different approach to development work and vouch for democracy and transparency. Experience has taught us that for a project to have long-term effects, it is crucial to have the beneficiaries partake in every aspect of the activities. Providing an avenue to share one of a kind gorgeous art and craftsmanship of hundreds of local immensely talented artisans. These artisans get an incredible opportunity to earn a source of income and change the quality of life for themselves and families by establishing a sustainable market for their unique handcrafted items thanks to passionate customers and fans like yourself.
